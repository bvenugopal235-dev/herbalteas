Our Story

Born in Nature,
Crafted with Love

HERBAL TEAS was born from a simple conviction: that nature holds every remedy we need. Founded in 2012 by a team of passionate herbalists and wellness advocates in the foothills of the Himalayas, we set out to bring the world's most potent botanical blends to your daily cup.

Over a decade later, our philosophy remains unchanged – source only the finest, respect the earth, and craft every blend with the intention of genuine healing. From high-altitude green tea gardens in Darjeeling to ancient spice farms in Kerala, every ingredient tells a story of dedication.
